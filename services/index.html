services we provide

Whether you need us to build your website from scratch, update an existing website, or provide website maintenance over a period of time, we can help. In addition to turnkey solutions, we offer the following services:

Content Management Systems

Ever wish you could edit any of the content on your site without needing help from a web designer? We can set you up with a CMS that lets you do just that, custom-tailored for anything you need to update. We have experience building sites in Drupal, Wordpress, Expression Engine & Magento.

Application Development

Need something more heavy-duty than a CMS can handle? No problem. We can build you a custom app that's fast, secure and extensible. We've created everything from billing applications to project management systems using our own in-house rapid application development framework.

Logo Design

A unique & communicative logo is an enormous asset to any company and its brand. We can create a design for you that evokes the feelings you're trying to elicit out of your customers every time they see it, and make it adaptable to any medium. We can even make business cards if you need them.

Graphic Design

Our graphic designs are more than just eye candy. They take advantage of usability principles like contrast and proximity to make your site's message easier to understand and guide your users where you ultimately want them to go - whether it's a contact page or a checkout process.

Information Architecture

During IA, we create interactive wireframes to emulate the site's flow. These mock-ups ensure the hierarchy & organization of content makes sense before any coding is done. A strong architecture can make even the most complex sites and web applications feel painless.

Front-End Templating

We take special care to make sure our HTML & CSS is written semantically which aids in both accessibility and search engine optimization. Our use of progressive enrichment ensures your more technologically-adept users get an enhanced experience without leaving everyone else behind.

Browser Compatibility

How compatible is your site with older browsers? One site's analytics may show 3% of users using Internet Explorer 6.0, whereas another might hit 70%. Regardless of the story your analytics tell, we have experience fixing all manner of cross-platform bugs. We can make sure it works in AOL 3.0, if you really want.

Usability Analysis

Are usability problems making your website or web application hard to use and driving customers away? Let us perform an analysis for you using up-to-date technologies like heatmaps and flow statistics, as well as our own usability expertise. We can help get you back on track.
